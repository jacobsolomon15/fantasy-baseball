Research Participant Information and Consent Form

Purpose of Research

This a research study about how people make decisions in fantasy sports games when using algorithmic tools.

  • You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this research.
  • You have been selected for this research because you passed the qualification quiz posted to mturk.com

What you will do

You will be asked to use a tool for analyzing baseball statistics and then to make predictions about the outcomes of some baseball games. Afterwards, you will be asked some basic demographic questions as well as some questions about your experience using the tool.

Potential Risks and Benefits

There are no direct potential benefits of participating in this research. However, this research may lead to improved tools and algorithms for helping decision makers in fantasy sports, medicine, finance, and other domains

There are no foreseeable risks associated with participation in this research

Your rights to participate, say no, or withdraw

Participation is voluntary. Refusal to participate will involve no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. You may discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. You have the right to say no. You may change your mind at any time and withdraw. You may choose not to answer specific questions or to stop participating at any time.

Costs and compensations for being in the study

There are no costs associated with the study. You will be compensated $0.75 for completing the study, which is expected to take 15 to 20 minutes. Additionally, you will receive a bonus payment for points earned in the fantasy sports game. These payment will be made through Amazon Mechanical Turk within 1 week of competing the study. We expect the average total compensation to be $2.00.
