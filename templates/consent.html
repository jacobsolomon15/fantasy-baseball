Research Participant Information and Consent Form

  • You are being asked to participate in a research study about how people evaluate automatically generated recommendations.
  • You will be asked to evaluate a set of recommendations which have been generated by an algorithmic recommender system. You will first be shown the criteria which are to be used when evaluating the recommendations. Next, you will be shown some information about how the recommender system works. Then, you will be shown some recommendations generated by the system. Finally, you will evaluate the recommendations according to the criteria which have been presented. Finally, you will be asked some basic demographic questions.
  • You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this research.

Your rights to participate, say no, or withdraw:

Participation in this research project is completely voluntary. You have the right to say no. You may change your mind at any time and withdraw. You may choose not to answer specific questions or to stop participating at any time.

Costs and Compensation for being in the study:

Participation in the study will take approximately 15 minutes.

You will receive $0.50 for completing the evaluation task. This payment will be made through your Amazon.com Mechanical Turk Account within 1 week of completing the task.
